Meet The Founders

Our Story

Perfect Gym was born in 2013 from a shared passion for health and fitness between childhood friends Rajesh Mehta and Vikram Patel. What started as a small training studio in Malad has grown into Mumbai's premier fitness destination.

After years of working in corporate jobs, Rajesh and Vikram decided to pursue their dream of creating a gym that would break the mold - a place that wasn't just about lifting weights, but about building a community focused on holistic wellness.

Do you Know Your Body Type?

On the basis of skeletal frame and body composition the human body can be classified into three major types: mainly ectomorph, mesomorph, and endomorph.

Ectomorph

Long and lean, with very less body fat, and muscles. Have a hard time gaining weight.

Mesomorph

Not overweight and not underweight, have won the genetic lottery by having a naturally muscular build and less of a tendency to store fat.

Endomorph

Lots of body fat, lots of muscle, and gain weight easily. They're heavier and rounder individuals and don't necessarily have to be overweight.
